Most Watched Directors

For my first visualization, I created a pie chart featuring the top 10 directors whose films I've watched.

Unfortunately, once I had my top ten directors list, I was unable to create a chart to compare their shares to the grand total as it would be too unseemly and too massive. Instead, I used the grand total of films from these ten directors and determined what percentage they made up from about 100 films.

It should be noted that these aren't my ten favorite directors, but just the ten directors whose filmographies I've most completed. Experienced veterans such as Steven Spielberg, Ridley Scott, and Martin Scorsese are unsurprisingly near the top as they are among my favorite directors as well as directors with some of the longest careers spanning multiple decades.
