Average Runtime Analysis

In this analysis, I wanted to determine whether I had any preference to watching longer films (over 120 mins) or shorter, more average-length, films (less than 120 mins).

After creating my visualization, I saw that the average runtime for a film I'm most likely to watch is about 100 mins (1hr 40mins), which is almost exactly what you'd expect the average length of a film to be (90 mins plus 10 mins of credits).

The graph is also pretty evenly distributed, so while I may be more inclined to knock out a shorter film, I'll gladly watch something in the 150 min to 200 min range as well.
